Mess Master

Abstract:

Managing a hostel mess tends to include multiple tasks such as organizing a proper schedule, keeping track of the number of members to be fed, and making sure to notify members regarding any changes in the schedule. The quality of food may also need to be tuned with regards to feedback provided by the consumers. A hostel owner can have a tough time staying on top of all the above-mentioned things, along with tracking the finances of the mess. To help with this, the Mess Management system hosted as a website introduces a platform for mess managers, hostel owners and members alike to integrate most, if not all tasks regarding managing and being part of a hostel mess. Additionally, it includes SMS integration, providing SMS alerts to all parties involved regarding any important changes to the functioning of the mess, as well as access to a payment gateway for all members to manage their fee paymentsÂ accordingly.
